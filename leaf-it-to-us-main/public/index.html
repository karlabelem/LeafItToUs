How to Use This Visualization

You can select different root attributes from the drop down menu and see how different plants are grouped together.

The upper nodes represent each unique plant, while their parent nodes represent how these plants are grouped togeher.

You can click on nodes to lock them in place. Click anywhere to close them before changing the root attribute.

Watering Needs

Watering requirements vary by plant type. Here's what they need:

  • Tropical Plants:
    • Peace Lily: Water frequently (every 4-7 days).
    • Boston Fern: Water frequently (every 4-7 days).
  • Succulents:
    • Aloe: Water rarely (every 3-4 weeks).
    • Snake Plant: Water occasionally (every 2-3 weeks).
    • Haworthia: Water rarely (every 3-4 weeks).
  • Temperate Plants:
    • English Ivy: Water moderately (every 1-2 weeks).
    • Calathea: Water moderately (every 1-2 weeks).

Ensure soil moisture aligns with the plantâ€™s needs to avoid over- or under-watering.

Light Requirements

Lighting needs depend on the plant group:

  • Tropical Plants:
    • Bird of Paradise: Full sun (6-8 hours daily).
    • Peace Lily: Medium light (moderate indirect sunlight).
    • Calathea: Partial shade (indirect or filtered light).
  • Succulents:
    • Aloe: Bright indirect light (prefers bright light but not direct sun).
    • Burro's Tail: Bright indirect light.
    • Snake Plant: Partial shade.
  • Temperate Plants:
    • Boston Fern: Partial shade.
    • English Ivy: Medium light.

Position your plants where they can get the right amount of light to thrive.
